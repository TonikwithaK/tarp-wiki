OpenArena uses id Tech 3 movement physics, the same base as Quake III Arena. Strafe jumping, rocket jumping, and plasma climbing are all available.

## Strafe Jumping

Combine directional movement with mouse turns to accelerate beyond normal run speed. Hold a strafe key, turn the mouse in the same direction, and jump repeatedly. Speed accumulates each jump. Used to traverse maps faster than walking.

## Rocket Jumping

Fire a Rocket Launcher at the ground while jumping to launch upward or forward. Self-damage is taken (up to 50 HP at close range). The Battle Suit powerup protects from splash self-damage.

## Plasma Climbing

Fire the Plasma Gun at a wall while moving toward it to climb vertically. The Plasma Gun's minor splash (radius 20, max 15 damage) provides upward momentum. Allows reaching areas that are otherwise inaccessible.

## Flight

The Flight powerup grants free flight for 60 seconds. Strafe jumping provides no speed bonus during Flight. Jump pads are ignored.
