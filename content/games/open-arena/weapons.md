OpenArena has 13 weapons. Players spawn with Gauntlet and Machine Gun. All others must be picked up.

Armor absorbs 66% of damage. Ammo cap per weapon: 200.

Weapons 11-13 (Nailgun, Prox Mine Launcher, Chaingun) originate from Quake III Team Arena.

## Notes

**Gauntlet:** Melee contact only. Earns Humiliation medal on kill. Cannot be selected with mouse scroll — press key 1.

**Machine Gun:** Starting weapon. 10 rounds/second. 2° spread.

**Shotgun:** 11 pellets per shot, 110 max damage at point blank. Hitscan.

**Grenade Launcher:** Bounces off walls. Detonates on player contact or after 2.5 seconds. Self-damage capped at 50 HP.

**Rocket Launcher:** Enables rocket jumping. Self-damage up to 50 HP. Aim at surfaces for splash rather than direct.

**Lightning Gun:** 160 DPS (8 dmg / 50ms), capped at 768 units range. Makes noise on weapon selection.

**Railgun:** 100% accurate. Slugs pass through multiple bodies. Two consecutive kills earns Impressive medal. Instantgib variant available.

**Plasma Gun:** Minor splash enables plasma climbing (radius 20, max 15 dmg).

**BFG10K:** ~5 shots/second. Self-damage up to 50 HP. Makes noise on selection. Often excluded from maps.

**Nailgun:** 15 nails per shot, 300 max damage. Slower than Shotgun, better at medium range.

**Prox Mine Launcher:** Sticky mines attach to walls, ceilings, players. Detonate on enemy proximity.

**Chaingun:** Fastest fire rate of any weapon. 30ms cycle.
