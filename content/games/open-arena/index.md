OpenArena is a free and open-source deathmatch FPS built on the ioquake3 engine (id Tech 3). It is a GPL-licensed clone of Quake III Arena, replacing all proprietary content with freely licensed equivalents. Free to download and play, no account required.

- **Engine:** ioquake3 (id Tech 3, OpenGL 1.x)
- **License:** GPLv2
- **Version:** 0.8.8 (February 2012); OAX beta active as of 2024
- **Platforms:** Windows, Linux, macOS, Android
- **Download:** [openarena.ws](http://openarena.ws/download.php?view.4)
