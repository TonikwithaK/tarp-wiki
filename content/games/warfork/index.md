# Warfork

Warfork is an open Arena FPS derived from Warsow. Its movement system — dash, walljump, strafe jump, air control — is the defining mechanical layer of the game.

Content here covers Warfork specifically. Nothing on these pages applies to Quake Live or Reflex Arena unless a dedicated cross-game comparison page says otherwise.
