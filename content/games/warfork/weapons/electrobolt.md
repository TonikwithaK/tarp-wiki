# Electrobolt

The Electrobolt is a single-shot, long-range hitscan weapon. It is the long-range weapon in the RL/LG/EB competitive triad.

## Stats

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Damage | 75 |
| Fire Rate | 1250ms cooldown |
| Self-Damage | 0 |
| Knockback | 80 |
| Stun | 1000 |
| Splash Radius | None |
| Travel Time | Instant (hitscan) |

## Mechanics

The Electrobolt fires a single hitscan beam that connects instantly at the moment of firing. There is no projectile and no travel time. Damage does not fall off at any range.

Each hit deals 75 damage, 80 knockback, and 1000 stun. There is no self-damage. Maximum ammo capacity is 10.

In weak mode, the Electrobolt deals the same 75 damage but knockback is reduced to 40.

## Counters

Closing distance during the 1250ms cooldown window forces the Electrobolt user into a less favorable range. Erratic movement, irregular jump patterns, and sudden direction changes make timing the shot harder. Breaking line of sight removes the threat entirely.
