# Team Deathmatch

Team Deathmatch (TDM) is a team-based frag mode. Players are divided into two teams: Alpha and Beta. Every kill any player lands scores one point for their team. The team with the highest combined frag count when time expires wins. If a score limit is set, the first team to reach it wins.

Respawning is continuous. There are no rounds.

---

## Limits

| Setting | Value |
|---|---|
| Score limit | 0 (disabled by default) |
| Time limit | 20 minutes |

---

| Item | Respawn |
|---|---|

| Mega Health | 20s (after holder drops to 100 HP) |
| Ultra Health | 40s |
| Weapon | 15s |
| Ammo | 20s |
| Powerup | 90s |

---

## Damage Rules

| Setting | Value |
|---|---|
| Self-damage | Enabled |
| Team-based | Yes (Alpha and Beta) |

---

## Scoring

Team score is the sum of all individual frags by players on that team.

---

## Distinction from Clan Arena

TDM has no rounds. Play is continuous, and individual frags accumulate into the team score throughout the match. In Clan Arena, rounds end when a team is fully eliminated and players do not respawn within a round.
