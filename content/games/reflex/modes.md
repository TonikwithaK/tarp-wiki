# Modes

The mode determines the match format (1v1, FFA, Race). The ruleset determines how weapons and movement behave. Modes and rulesets are set independently.

## Duel

Match length: 10 minutes in Competitive and Sushi, 5 minutes in Casual. Standard 1v1 maps use a 1 Red Armor / 2 Yellow Armor / 1 Mega Health item layout.

## Race

Players phase through each other. Runs are timed from start to finish. The `suicide` command resets position. 589 workshop maps, the third largest pool, larger than TDM.

## Arena Modes

Elimination format. Players spawn with 200 armor and 100 health and all weapons at round start. No item pickups during rounds. Rounds end when one side is fully eliminated. A 90-second damage tick begins after the time limit expires.

## CTF

Carnage powerup spawns every 90 seconds, typically near map center.
