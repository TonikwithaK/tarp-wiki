# Powerups

Quake Live has two primary powerups: Quad Damage and Regeneration. Not all maps include powerups. Powerup availability depends on map and gametype configuration.

## Quad Damage

Quad Damage multiplies the carrier's outgoing damage by 4 for its duration (30 seconds). It is a map pickup at a fixed spawn location. The carrier is visually marked with a blue glow. All weapons deal quadrupled damage while active.

The respawn timer is 60 seconds after pickup.

## Regeneration

Regeneration heals the carrier over time for its duration. Less common than Quad on standard competitive maps.

## Haste

Haste increases the carrier's movement speed and weapon fire rate for its duration. Present on some maps.

|---|---|---|
