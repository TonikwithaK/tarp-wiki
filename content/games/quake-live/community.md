# Community

## quakelife.ru

Russian QL and Arena FPS community hub. Active since ~2011. Hosts the 250 FPS QL Duel League, LAN events, and a set of reference tools.

Telegram: [@quakelife](https://t.me/quakelife)

## Spawn Visualizer

Interactive HTML5/JS duel spawn point visualizations by Memento_Mori (March 2015). Shows which spawn points activate based on player positions and frag counts.

https://quakelife.ru/tools/ql-spawns/

Covered maps: aerowalk, battleforged, bloodrun, campgrounds, campgroundsintel, cure, furiousheights, hektik, lostworld, silence, sinister, terminatria, toxicity, windsongkeep

---

## Config Collection

Community-maintained collection of configs, HUDs, and scripts from professional and notable players. Sourced from streams and tournaments.

https://quakelife.ru/tools/ql-configs/

GitHub: https://github.com/Danmer/ql-configs

Steam Workshop (auto-sync): https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=863455073

Contains player configs, HUD configs, and scripts. Open for community uploads (submitted configs become public domain).
