# News and Community

Community coverage, tournament results, and competitive news for Quake Live.

---

## News and Coverage

**[Plusforward](https://www.plusforward.net)**
Defacto source for Quake Live competitive rankings, tournament results, and community news. Also covers Quake Champions. The place to check for current standings, event announcements, and match coverage.

**[ESReality](https://www.esreality.com)**
Long-running AFPS community site. Quake tournament coverage, pro player posts, and community discussion going back to the early 2000s. Thinner on day-to-day news now but still active for major events.

**[QuakeLive.com](https://www.quakelive.com)**
Official site. Patch notes and seasonal updates. Login required for profile access.

---

## Streaming

**[Twitch: quakelive](https://www.twitch.tv/quakelive)**
Official channel. Broadcasts major tournaments when they run.

Search `quakelive` or `quake live` on Twitch for community streams.

**[YouTube: Quake](https://www.youtube.com/c/QuakeChampions)**
Bethesda's Quake channel covers both QL and QC events. VODs of major tournaments.

---

## Forums and Discussion

**[Steam Discussions: Quake Live](https://steamcommunity.com/app/282440/discussions/)**
Active player discussion. Settings help, server issues, pickup game coordination.

**[Reddit: r/QuakeLive](https://www.reddit.com/r/QuakeLive/)**
Lighter traffic but still used for news and questions.

**[ESReality Forums](https://www.esreality.com/?a=forums)**
Older community. Competitive discussion, tech help.

---

## Competitive Rankings

**[Plusforward Rankings](https://www.plusforward.net/quakelive/)**
Current global duel and CA rankings. Most reliable active tracking for QL matchmaking skill levels.
