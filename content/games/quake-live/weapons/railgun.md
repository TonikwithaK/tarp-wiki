The Railgun (RG, Rail) fires a hitscan beam dealing 80 damage per shot. No splash damage. No self-damage. Unlimited range. Reload time is approximately 1.5 seconds. Respawn time is 5 seconds.

## Stats

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Damage per shot | 80 |
| Splash damage | None |
| Self-damage | None |
| Range | Unlimited |
| Reload time | ~1.5 seconds |
| Respawn time | 5 seconds |

## Mechanics

The Rail fires instantly on click with no travel time. It deals a fixed 80 damage to the first player it hits regardless of range. In standard play the beam does not penetrate walls. The shot passes through walls only in specific gametype configurations.
