# Shotgun

The Shotgun fires a spread of pellets dealing damage across a close-range cone. High damage at point-blank range, significant falloff at distance. Respawn time is 5 seconds.

## Mechanics

Multiple pellets fire per shot in a fixed spread pattern. Each pellet deals a fraction of the total possible damage. At close range the majority of pellets connect, giving high burst damage. At distance the spread causes most pellets to miss.

## Role in Play

The Shotgun is a situational close-range weapon. In open competitive play on standard maps it is rarely the focus weapon, but it can deal significant burst damage when an opponent is at point-blank range and the primary triad weapons are on cooldown or out of ammo.
