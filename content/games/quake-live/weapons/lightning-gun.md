The Lightning Gun (LG) fires a continuous hitscan beam dealing 6-7 damage per tick at a high fire rate. No splash damage. No self-damage. Effective range is capped: beam does not connect beyond approximately 768 units. Respawn time is 5 seconds.

## Stats

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Damage per tick | 6-7 |
| Splash damage | None |
| Self-damage | None |
| Max range | ~768 units |
| Respawn time | 5 seconds |

## Mechanics

The LG beam connects instantly on every tick as long as the crosshair is on the target and the target is within range. Damage accumulates continuously while the beam is active and on target. Because it is hitscan, no leading is required.

## Effective Range

The LG has a hard range cutoff. At distances beyond the cap the beam misses regardless of aim.
