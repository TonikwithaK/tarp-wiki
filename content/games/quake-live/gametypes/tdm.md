## Format

Two teams. Team score is the sum of individual frags. Continuous respawn. Most team frags by time limit wins.

## Mode Settings

| Setting | Value | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Time limit | 20 minutes | Base default, not overridden by standard factory |
| Score limit | 0 | Disabled by default |

## Spawn

Machine Gun + 125 HP. All weapons and items acquired from map pickups.

Item pickups present.
