# Gametypes

Quake Live ships with multiple gametypes. Competitive and community play is concentrated in a subset of them.

## Competitive Gametypes

### [[duel|Duel]]

1v1. Players start with Machine Gun and 125 HP. All weapons and items are acquired from map pickups. Default time limit is 10 minutes.

### [[clan-arena|Clan Arena]]

Team elimination. Players spawn with all standard weapons and full health and armor each round. No item pickups. Rounds end when one team is fully eliminated.

### [[ffa|Free For All (FFA)]]

Deathmatch. All players compete individually. Item pickups on the map. Default time limit is 20 minutes.

### [[tdm|Team Deathmatch (TDM)]]

Two teams. Frags score for the team. Item pickups on the map. Default time limit is 20 minutes.

### [[ctf|Capture the Flag (CTF)]]

Two teams. Players steal the enemy flag and return it to their base. Default time limit is 20 minutes.

## All Official Gametypes

| Gametype | Abbreviation | Format | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| Free For All | FFA | Solo | All-versus-all deathmatch. Item pickups on the map. |
| Duel | — | 1v1 | One-on-one. Players start with Machine Gun and 125 HP. All items acquired from map. |
| Race | — | Solo | Players complete a course. Scored by fastest completion time. |
| Team Deathmatch | TDM | Team | Two teams. Frags score for the team. Item pickups on the map. |
| Clan Arena | CA | Team | Team elimination. Players spawn with all weapons, full health and armor. No item pickups. |
| Capture the Flag | CTF | Team | Two teams. Capture the enemy flag and return it to base. |
| One-Flag CTF | 1FCTF | Team | Variant of CTF with a single neutral flag. Teams fight to capture it and bring it to the enemy base. |
| Harvester | — | Team | Players collect skulls dropped by kills and deposit them at a central point. |
| Domination | DOM | Team | Teams capture and hold control points on the map. |
| Freeze Tag | FTAG | Team | Players are frozen on death rather than respawning. Teammates can unfreeze frozen players. |
| Attack and Defend | A&D | Team | Asymmetric objective mode. One team attacks, one team defends. |
| Red Rover | — | Team | Players switch teams when killed. Team with all players wins the round. |

## Notes

Pickup games for CTF and TDM are organized through community Discord servers rather than public matchmaking.
