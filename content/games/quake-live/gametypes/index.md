# Gametypes

Quake Live ships with multiple gametypes. Competitive and community play is concentrated in a subset of them.

## Competitive Gametypes

### Duel

1v1. Players start with Machine Gun and 125 HP. All weapons and items are acquired from map pickups. Default time limit is 10 minutes.

### Clan Arena

Team elimination. Players spawn with all standard weapons and full health and armor each round. No item pickups. Rounds end when one team is fully eliminated.

### Free For All

Deathmatch. All players compete individually. Item pickups on the map. Default time limit is 20 minutes.

### Team Deathmatch

Two teams. Frags score for the team. Item pickups on the map. Default time limit is 20 minutes.

### Capture the Flag

Two teams. Players steal the enemy flag and return it to their base. Default time limit is 20 minutes.

## Additional Gametypes

**Freeze Tag:** Team mode. Players are frozen on death rather than respawning. Teammates can unfreeze frozen players.

**Domination:** Teams capture and hold control points on the map.

**Attack and Defend:** Asymmetric objective mode. One team attacks, one defends.

**Race:** Players complete a course. Scored by fastest completion time.

**Harvester:** Players collect skulls dropped by kills and deposit them at a central point.

## Notes

Pickup games for CTF and TDM are organized through community Discord servers rather than public matchmaking.
